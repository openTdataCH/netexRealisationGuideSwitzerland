185.8. The group netex:FlexiblePointPropertiesGroup

netex:FlexiblePointPropertiesGroup

Elements for a FLEXIBLE POINT PROPERTies. Especially such POINT may stand in for a whole FLEXIBLE ZONE.

The element contains none or one of the following elements (a-b)
netex:FlexiblePointPropertiesGroup

a

→‍netex:PointOnRouteRef

-1:1

>netex:PointOnRouteRefStructure

Reference to POINT ON ROUTE.

b

→‍netex:PointRef

-1:1

>netex:PointRefStructure

Reference to a POINT.

netex:MayBeSkipped

0:1

Whether the POINT may be skipped.

netex:OnMainRoute

0:1

Whether the POINT is on the main ROUTE.

netex:PointStandingForAZone

0:1

Whether the POINT represents a FLEXIBLE ZONE.

netex:ZoneContainingStops

0:1

Whether the ZONE is defined by a GROUP of POINT (true) or a geographical zone defined by its boundary.
